## SNES9X Configuration

SNES9X is other example of an emulator that lets you change paths. So basically make whichever folders you want (saves, states, cheats...) in the Syncthing folder and then point the emulator paths to the folders you created. 

This emulator is one example of what I meant in the readme, which doesn't have a community parser in Steam ROM Manager, so in my case I took RetroArch one and modified it with the Flatpak image name and added a couple parameters.
